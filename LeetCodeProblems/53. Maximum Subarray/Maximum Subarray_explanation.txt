my intution:


- assume we are already at the middle of the array, given an index i, we can either add arr[i] to the current subarray
  or create our own new subarray starting from arr[i]

- to determine that we simply ask which is bigger, arr[i] on its own or arr[i] + prev subarray
- if arr[i] is bigger theres no need to append it to the prev subarray cus that would make the total sum lower than if
  we were to use arr[i] on its own, so make a new subarray that starts with arr[i]
  otherwise we add arr[i] to the prev subarray


ex: 1 2 3 4 5 -3 -2 10
when we reach -3 we ask: is it bigger to have -3 on its own or do we add it to our previous subarray
the answer would be to add it to prev subarray.

of course we keep track with the largest subarray we met yet by comparing each prev_subarray with our current max sub array
